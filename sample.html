Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our cybersecurity solutions for financial institutions.

What compliance standards do you support?

We support all major financial compliance standards including PCI-DSS, GDPR, SOC 2, ISO 27001, RBI guidelines for banks, SEBI regulations, and local financial authority requirements. Our platform automates evidence collection and reporting for these standards.

How quickly can you respond to security incidents?

Our SLA guarantees a 60-minute response time for critical incidents affecting financial operations. We have a dedicated team of financial security specialists available 24/7/365 to respond to threats targeting banking systems, payment processors, and financial data.

Do you offer customized solutions for specific financial sectors?

Yes, we provide tailored solutions for retail banking, investment firms, insurance companies, payment processors, and fintech startups. Each sector has unique security requirements, and our team has deep expertise in securing all types of financial institutions.

How do you handle data residency requirements for banks?

We offer regional data hosting options to comply with local financial regulations. All sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with keys managed by your institution. Our architecture supports private cloud, hybrid, and on-premises deployments for maximum flexibility.
